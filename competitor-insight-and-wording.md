## What we learned from competitor visual/positioning patterns
1. **Trust has to be visible in the hero zone**
   - Competitors lead with emergency availability, certifications, service areas, and direct contact.
   - Update applied: all 12 options open with a clear trust action path in or near the first viewport.

2. **Claims support is expected, not premium**
   - Review pages repeatedly mention claim support and adjuster workflow.
   - Update applied: every option includes at least one clear insurance/claim checkpoint before conversion CTA.

3. **Local service footprint builds social proof faster than generic branding**
   - Regional names (Southport, Oak Island, Brunswick, etc.) are used heavily by competitors.
   - Update applied: local footprint language appears consistently in options and the new `Neighborhood Trust Hub` and `Coastal Emergency Calm` concepts.

4. **Fortified and standards language is powerful in coastal markets**
   - MOcean-style positioning around FORTIFIED and grant opportunities performs for coastal homeowners.
   - Update applied: fortified and durable-weather framing appears across modern and premium offerings so differentiation is stronger than price alone.

5. **People want calm, not alarmist, urgency for storm leads**
   - Emergency-first competitors can feel aggressive and can reduce trust.
   - Update applied: `Coastal Emergency Calm` and `Emergency Command` frame urgency as controlled steps.

## Humanizer AI pass used for wording
- Converted hard technical copy into conversational sentences while preserving decision-critical details.
- Replaced abstract claims with concrete homeowner outcomes (safety, clarity, predictability, cleanup confidence).
- Added plain-language sequencing blocks (“Minute 15,” “Hour 1,” “Day 1–3,” etc.) to reduce cognitive load.
- Replaced over-adjective language with practical assurance statements tied to action.

## Why each new style exists
- **Option 10 — Coastal Emergency Calm**: for storm-sensitive families who need urgency with emotional containment.
- **Option 11 — Neighborhood Trust Hub**: for referral-heavy local markets where community proof matters more than pitch.
- **Option 12 — Quote Clarity Station**: for practical buyers comparing options and asking for structure before commitment.
