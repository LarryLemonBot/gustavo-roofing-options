Using the roof gallery

Are these Vera's Roofing job photos?

Yes. The gallery uses Vera's Roofing job photos and roof-detail photos so property owners can compare real rooflines, repairs, prep, metal, copper, and EPDM details before they call.

Can I send a photo that looks similar to one in the gallery?

Yes. Text the similar photo, town, property type, roof or gutter concern, and photos if available.

What if I do not know the roof type?

You do not need to know the roof type before reaching out. Send a few clear photos and describe the issue in plain language.

Do photos confirm what needs to be repaired?

No. Photos help start the conversation, but inspection details, roof age, access, slope, material, and condition determine the right repair or replacement plan.
