# Vera Roofing Website Review Package

Public review URL:

https://larrylemonbot.github.io/gustavo-roofing-options/

## Purpose

This package is a curated website direction review for Vera Roofing in Brunswick County, North Carolina.
It uses real job imagery and focuses the brand around owner-inspected workmanship, fortified roofing,
storm response, insurance-claim support, copper details, cedar shake, EPDM systems, and warranty-forward
positioning.

## Primary Directions

- `Luxury Assurance`: premium coastal positioning for high-trust homeowners.
- `Emergency Command`: storm damage and insurance-claim response.
- `Neighborhood Trust`: local referral and word-of-mouth confidence.
- `Copper Craft`: craft, copper detail, and specialty-material positioning.

## Secondary Directions

- `Heritage Coastal`: established, warm, traditional trust.
- `Proof-First Premium`: proof-led structure and premium reassurance.
- `Coastal Emergency Calm`: calm storm-response framing.
- `Quote Clarity`: practical decision support and clear next steps.

## Before Production

- Confirm the final public business name and logo.
- Confirm phone, email, service area, and emergency availability.
- Verify the exact warranty language before publishing.
- Add real testimonials, Google review snippets, and final licensing/legal language.
- Replace any temporary photos with Gustavo-approved finished-project images.
