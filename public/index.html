Roofing questions

What should I text Vera's Roofing?

Text a few clear roof photos, the town, property type, and what you are seeing. Mention whether the concern is a leak, storm damage, aging shingles, metal or copper detail, low-slope waterproofing, or replacement planning.

Can photos replace a roof inspection?

Photos help start a practical roofing conversation, but they do not replace every inspection need. Vera's Roofing can use photos to understand the concern and talk through the next practical step.

What areas does Vera's Roofing work across?

Vera's Roofing works across Brunswick County, Wilmington, New Hanover County, and nearby coastal North Carolina communities. If your town is not listed, text the town and roof photos and ask whether Vera's Roofing can help.

Do rental and business-property owners use the same photo-first process?

Yes. Homeowners, rental-property owners, and business-property owners can text photos with the town, property type, roof concern, and access notes so the first conversation is easier to follow.
