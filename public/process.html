Process questions

What to expect before, during, and after the roof work.

Does every Vera's Roofing job include hand-nailing?

No. Hand-nailing is only part of the work when it is included in the agreed scope. When hand-nailing is part of the scope, each fastener can be placed deliberately and checked for placement and depth.

Can every roof receive CertainTeed manufacturer warranty coverage?

No. CertainTeed warranty availability depends on the selected materials, full roof system, installation requirements, registration, property type, transfer rules, and CertainTeed's written terms. Vera's Roofing can talk through current manufacturer warranty options before materials are ordered.

Can Vera's Roofing discuss FORTIFIED Home requirements?

If FORTIFIED Home™ requirements matter for your roof, ask early. Gustavo Vera has completed the 2025 FORTIFIED Standard - Roofing Contractor Exam, but any designation still depends on eligible scope, documentation, evaluator verification, current program rules, and project-specific review. No FORTIFIED designation or insurance benefit is promised.

What does owner-led final review mean?

The owner reviews finished-work photos and key details at the end of the job so the final walkthrough is clear.

Do photos confirm a repair plan?

No. Photos help start the roofing conversation, but inspection details, roof age, access, slope, material, and condition determine the right repair or replacement plan.
