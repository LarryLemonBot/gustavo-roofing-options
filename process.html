Process questions

What to expect before, during, and after the roof work.

Does every Vera's Roofing job include hand-nailing?

No. Hand-nailing is only part of the work when it is included in the agreed scope. When hand-nailing is part of the scope, it gives the crew more control over shingle nail placement and nail depth.

What does owner-led final review mean?

The owner reviews finished-work photos and key details at the end of the job so the final walkthrough is clear.

Do photos confirm a repair plan?

No. Photos help start the roofing conversation, but inspection details, roof age, access, slope, material, and condition determine the right repair or replacement plan.
